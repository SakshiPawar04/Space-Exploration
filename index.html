About Space Exploration

Space exploration is the ongoing discovery of celestial structures in outer space by means of evolving space technology. It involves studying planets, stars, galaxies, and the universe as a whole to understand our place in the cosmos.

It has the potential to solve scientific mysteries, inspire future generations, and innovate new technologies.
